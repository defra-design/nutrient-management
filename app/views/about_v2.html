About this tool

This tool helps to create nutrient management plans, Nitrate Vulnerable Zone (NVZ) reports, and to estimate the nutrient supply from organic materials.

To use the features of this tool you will need:

  • farm address
  • an average rainfall figure (if you have one)
  • the sizes of your fields (in hectares)
  • crop details, including yields and sowing dates (if you have them)
  • manure application details, including application rate, application method and soil incorporation

Nutrient management plans require:

  • your most recent soil analysis data
  • last year's crop type for each field
  • details of how grass fields have been managed in the last three years

Nutrient management plans include Soil Nitrogen Supply (SNS). You can add SNS data for a field at the end of the planning process, if you have it.

Some NVZ reports also require:

  • livestock details
  • manure storage details

The tool will warn you of possible breaches of the NVZ rules as you make nutrient management plans, or estimate nutrient supply from organic materials (using MANNER-NPK).

Saving your data

The tool allows you to save your data, edit it, and reuse it. You can also save or print plans, reports and estimates as PDF files.
