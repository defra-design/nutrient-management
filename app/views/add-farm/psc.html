Phosphorus sorption capacity (PSC)

The phosphorus sorption capacity (PSC) of soil is its ability to bind with applied phosphorus.

If you are unsure about the PSC for your soiil, the environment agency in scotland provides a map of soil PSC indices.

View the map (opens in a new window)

You can also read the SRUC Technical Notes TN715 to TN718, and TN770 (specific to your area) on the Farm Advisory Service Website.

View the SRUC technical notes (opens in a new window)

If you are still unsure, use PSC Index 1.
