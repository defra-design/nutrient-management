Low risk run-off land

You can spread slurry or poultry manure on fields that have a low risk of run-off, to reduce the amount of storage capacity you need to provide.

Low run-off risk land is land that:

  • has an average slope of less than 3 degrees
  • does not have land drains (other than a sealed pipe)
  • is at least 50 metres from a watercourse or conduit leading to a watercourse
