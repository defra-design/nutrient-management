About the default values

Occupancy is the proportion of the year that the animal place is occupied by animals.

The remaining time will be for cleaning etc.

If the occupancy on your farm is different to the occupancy values shown here, you should enter the actual occupancy figures for your farm.

If you enter your own nitrogen (N) standard figure, do not change the occupancy value.
