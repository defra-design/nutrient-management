Conditions affecting the loss of nutrients from your organic materials

About these values

The effectiveness of your organic material application and the amount of inorganic fertiliser you need will vary depending on these conditions.

Autumn crop nitrogen (N) uptake is the amount of Nitrogen(N) from the application taken up by the crop in the autumn.

End of soil drainage is the date when soil drainage ends and the soil begins to dry out.

Rainfall within 6 hours of application is the amount of rain that falls in the 6 hours after the material is applied.

Effective rainfall after application is the average rainfall between manure application and end of soil drainage.

Windspeed during application is the windspeed when the material is applied.

Topsoil moisture is how moist the topsoil is when the material is applied.
