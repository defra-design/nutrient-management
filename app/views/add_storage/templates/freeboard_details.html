About freeboard

Freeboard is the distance between the top of the store and the level of the slurry inside. You must always keep this distance clear.

{% if data.storage_type == 'earth banked' %} For earth-banked lagoons, you must keep a minimum of 75cm of freeboard. {% else %} For a steel or concrete store, you must keep a minimum of 30cm of freeboard. {% endif %}
