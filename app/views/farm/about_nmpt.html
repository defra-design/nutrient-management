What you will need

To create nutrient management plans and Nitrate Vulnerable Zone (NVZ) reports you will need:

  • farm name, address and postcode
  • the sizes of your fields in hectares
  • crop details, including yields and sowing dates if you have them
  • manure application details, including application rate, application method and soil incorporation
  • your most recent soil analysis data
  • last year's crop type for each field
  • details of how grass fields have been managed in the last three years

Some NVZ reports also require:

  • livestock details
  • manure storage details

Soil Nitrogen Supply (SNS)

Nutrient management plans include SNS. You can add SNS data for a field at the end of the planning process, if you have it.

Nitrate Vulnerable Zone (NVZ) warnings

The tool will warn you of possible breaches of the NVZ rules as you make nutrient management plans.

Saving your data

You can save your data, edit it, and reuse it. You can also save or print plans and reports as PDF files.
