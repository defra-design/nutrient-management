How estimating nutrient supply from organic materials works using MANNER-NPK

MANNER-NPK (Manure Nitrogen Evaluation Routine) estimates the nutrient supply from applications of organic materials.

The estimate will show you:

  • total nitrogen (N)
  • nitrogen (N) losses
  • crop available nitrogen (N)
  • total and crop available phosphate (P2O5), potash (K2O), sulphur (SO3) and magnesium (MgO)

What you'll need

  • farm and field details, you can use any previously entered
  • manure type and nutrient content (laboratory analysis results or you can use default nutrient content)
  • manure application details, including application rate, application method and soil incorporation

Economic value of organic materials

You can calculate the potential financial value of your manure application(s) based on fertiliser prices. You will be able to input your prices for inorganic fertilisers.

This will help you understand the cost savings you could make by using manure instead of buying inorganic fertilisers.

Saving and printing

You can save your estimates as a PDF and print them.
