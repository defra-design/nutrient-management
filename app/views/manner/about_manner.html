About nutrient supply estimates (using MANNER-NPK)

You can use this tool to create an estimate of the nutrients supplied from applications of organic materials.

Estimates show you:

  • total nitrogen (N)
  • crop available nitrogen (N)
  • nitrogen (N) losses
  • total, and crop available phosphate (P2O5), potash (K2O), sulphur (SO3) and magnesium (MgO)
  • the potential value of the organic material, when compared to the cost of inorganic fertilisers

What you will need

  • farm name, address and postcode
  • manure application details, including application rate, application method and soil incorporation

Nitrate Vulnerable Zone (NVZ) warnings

The tool will warn you of possible breaches of the NVZ rules as you create estimates.

Saving your data

You can save your data, edit it, and reuse it. You can also save or print estimates as PDF files.

You can manually add the results from an estimate, to a nutrient management plan, if you need to.
