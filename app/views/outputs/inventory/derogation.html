About derogations

You can apply up to 170kg per hectare of nitrogen in livestock manure on your holding in a nitrate vulnerable zone (NVZ) in each calendar year. This limit is the loading limit and applies as an average across your holding.

You can apply for grassland derogation to use up to 250kg of nitrogen per hectare from grazing livestock manure.

You can only apply for a grassland derogation if at least 80% of the agricultural area of your holding is grass.

You must comply with the conditions of a derogation and the NVZ rules if your application is successful.

More information: Grassland derogations for livestock manure in nitrate vulnerable zones (opens in a new window)
