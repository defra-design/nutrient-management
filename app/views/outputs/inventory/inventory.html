NVZ minimum storage requirements

About NVZ minimum storage requirements

The NVZ Action Programme requires that there is a minimum storage capacity requirement for livestock slurries and poultry manures.

The minimum storage capacity for slurry must include the volume of slurry collected and any dilution water such as wash water and rainfall that is collected in the slurry store during October to March (pig or poultry slurry) or October to February (cattle/other livestock slurry), allowing for exports of slurry.

The minimum storage requirement for poultry manure is 6 months production (October to March), allowing for imports and exports.

For further information see GOV.UK
