Clean rainfall is the quantity of rainfall landing on clean concrete and roofs, or uncovered slurry stores that drain to a slurry store. This quantity of rainfall may be easily redirected to reduce the volume of slurry.

Dirty rainfall is the quantity of rainfall landing on fouled open concrete, and uncovered solid manure stores, silage clamps, etc., that drain to a manure store.
