About the grassland derogation

You can apply up to 170kg per hectare of nitrogen (N) in livestock manure on your holding in a nitrate vulnerable zone (NVZ) in each calendar year.

This is the nitrogen (N) loading limit and applies as an average across your holding.

You can apply for a grassland derogation to apply up to 250kg of
nitrogen (N) per hectare of manure from grazing livestock.

You must comply with the conditions of a derogation and the NVZ rules if your application is successful.

More information: Grassland derogations for livestock manure in nitrate vulnerable zones (opens in a new window)
