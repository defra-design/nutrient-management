What is the Phosphorus sorption capacity (PSC) index of Top Field?

Help with Phosphorus sorption capacity (PSC)

The phosphorus sorption capacity (PSC) of soil is its ability to bind with applied phosphorus.

If you are unsure about the PSC for your soil, the Scottish Environment Protection Agency (SEPA) provides a map of soil PSC indices.
