Farm average yield adjustment

The N-max limit is based on standard yield values for these crops:

  • Spring Barley
  • Winter wheat

You can adjust the N-max limit for these crops by entering a farm average yield, if you have one.

Help with farm average yield adjustment

A farm average yield adjustment must be based on either:

  • a yield previously achieved for the crop at {{data.farm.name}}, or
  • evidence from another farm, if a new crop management system is being used

If you are using evidence from another farm, it must come from the same nitrate vulnerable zone (NVZ), with a similar soil type. It must show the soil nutrient values where yields were achieved from the same crop, using the same crop management system.

You must have at least 3 years of records to demonstrate the actual yields achieved.

If you do not want to use a farm average yield for a crop, leave it blank
