{{ serviceName }}

This is a free tool, provided by Defra, to help farmers, land managers and advisers plan and record nutrient use on their land. It gives you guidance only - there is no legal requirement to use it.

This service will:

  • save you time and money
  • help you comply with environmental regulations
  • help protect the environment

You must follow the appropriate regulations, depending on where your farm is.

How it works

The recommendations from this service come from RB209 Nutrient Management Guide. (opens in a new window).

It gives fertiliser recommendations for all major nutrients and lime, and for most agricultural and horticultural crops.

Your plan will be based on:

  • the crops you grow and their nutrient requirements
  • the nutrients available, soil nitrogen supply and other soil analysis results
  • the organic materials and inorganic fertilisers you use and how you apply them

You can develop a nutrient application plan during the season. This tool will keep detailed records of cropping, soil analyses, and each fertiliser and manure application.

It creates downloadable plans in PDF.

It replaces earlier tools such as PLANET and MANNER. The tool will allow you to import your data from PLANET.

Your data

We only gather the data required to provide you with the appropriate recommendations.

This tool will never share your data with any other organisations.

In particular, please note that this tool does not share data with the Rural Payments Agency (RPA).

This service was developed in conjunction with ADAS, AHDB and RSK.
