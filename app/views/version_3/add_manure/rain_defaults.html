Conditions affecting the loss of nutrients from your organic materials

These values are used to calculate how effective this organic material application is.

About these values

Autumn crop Nitrogen uptake

Amount of N from the application taken up by the crop in the autumn.

End of soil drainage

Date when soil drainage ends and the soil begins to dry out.

Rainfall within 6 hours of application

Amount of rain that falls in the 6 hours after the material is applied.

Effective rainfall after application

Average rainfall between manure application and end of soil drainage.

Windspeed during application

Windspeed when the material is applied.

Topsoil moisture

How moist the topsoil is when the material is applied.
