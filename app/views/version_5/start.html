{{ serviceName }}

This is a free service, provided by Defra, to help farmers plan and record nutrient use on their farms. It gives you guidance only - there is no legal requirement to use it.

This service will:

  • save you time and money
  • help you comply with environmental regulations
  • help protect the environment

How it works

The recommendations from this service come from the Nutrient Management Guide (RB209).

Your plan will be based on:

  • the crops you grow
  • the nutrients in your soil
  • the organic materials and inorganic fertilisers you use and how you apply them

You can download your plans as PDFs.

This service replaces earlier tools such as PLANET and MANNER. The service will allow you to import your data from PLANET.

Sustainable Farming Incentive (SFI)

In order to apply for the NUM1 payment of the SFI, you will need a nutrient plan. This service will create a plan that you can use for this purpose.

Please note that you will then need to get the plan checked by a FACTS qualified adviser before applying.

Your data

We only gather the data required to provide you with the appropriate recommendations.

This service will never share your data with any other organisations.

In particular, please note that this service does not share data with the Rural Payments Agency (RPA).

This service was developed in conjunction with ADAS and AHDB.
