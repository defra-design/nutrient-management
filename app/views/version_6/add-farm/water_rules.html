Farming Rules for Water

All agricultural land in England is subject to the Farming Rules for Water.

The rules aim to reduce and prevent diffuse water pollution from agricultural sources.

To comply with the rules a land manager must ensure that all applications of organic manure or manufactured fertiliser are planned so that they don’t exceed the needs of the soil and crop on that land or give rise to a significant risk of agricultural diffuse pollution.

Creating a nutrient management plan will help you comply with these rules.
