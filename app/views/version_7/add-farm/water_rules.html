Farming Rules for Water

All agricultural land in England is subject to the Farming Rules for Water.

These rules aim to reduce and prevent water pollution from agricultural sources.

To comply with the rules, you must ensure that all applications of organic material or inorganic fertiliser are planned. This is so they do not exceed the needs of the soil and crop on that land, or create a significant risk of pollution.

Creating and following a nutrient management plan will help you comply with these rules.
