Guidance

Nitrogen

Apply no more than 100kg N/ha at sowing or transplanting then apply N according to crop growth, e.g. up to 60kg N/ha per month in the south-west and 20kg N/ha in the north. Where seedbed SNS exceeds 4 and crops are likely to be harvested in April or later, top-dress N at the start of spring growth allowing for any over-winter losses of N.
