This service is currently in a private beta phase. This means that we are testing the service with a group of invited users. As such, some functionality may not work as expected and some features have not yet been developed.

Check the release schedule to see what features have been added to the test version of the service.

About recommendations, guidance, and advice

This service was developed by the Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs (Defra), the Agriculture and Horticulture Development Board (AHDB), ADAS and the RSK group. Nutrient recommendations are derived from RB209 Application Programming Interface (API); RB209 is the intellectual property of AHDB.

While Defra and partners seek to ensure that the information provided by this service is accurate at the time of use, Defra and partners accept no liability for loss, damage or injury howsoever caused (including that caused by negligence) or suffered directly or indirectly in relation to information contained in or omitted from this service.

Users are responsible for the accuracy of their own data and the application of guidance provided. This service should not be relied upon as the sole source of nutrient management guidance. Always consider local conditions and, if necessary, consult a FACTS Qualified Adviser before making nutrient management decisions.

Moving your data to the live service

If you wish to take your farm data and plans from the testing version to the live version, please email our team at helpdesk@rsk-bsl.co.uk.

We will migrate your data for you when the service launches, and then your plans will be suitable for regulatory use.

Getting help

If you experience a technical problem with any of the functionality in this service, you can email: helpdesk@rsk-bsl.co.uk.

The helpdesk will help with issues such as:

  • technical issues
  • login issues
  • lservice outages

Please tell us what you were trying to do, what happened, and what you expected. This will help us to identify the problem.

Help with the service's recommendations

You can email the helpdesk if you have any issues with the data and recommendations the service provides. Email: helpdesk@rsk-bsl.co.uk.

This service might give slightly different recommendations from the previous service PLANET. This is because this new service uses an updated version of the RB209 API, so it provides the very latest data.

We will reply to your email as quickly as we can.
